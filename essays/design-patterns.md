## Should we reinvent the wheel?

Imagine a life without the invention of the wheel. Hard to picture, right? Fortunately, you don't have to. The wheel has already been invented! The wheel is possibly the greatest invention that humans have created. Chances are that you use or see a wheel every day, either as transportation on a car or skateboard, for comfort as an electric fan, for tools such as a drill or a pizza cutter, or for watching your favorite televeision game show, "Wheel of Fortune". While not exactly a one-to-one function, the same case can be applied to software design. In this case, the "wheel" is called "design patterns". Let's say you have a problem during your software development that you swear you have encountered before. A design pattern would be your solution. Like the wheel, a design pattern is essentially a template that can be used for a multitude of situations.
